## 3. Standard Operating Procedures

### 3.1 Daily Operations Flow

**Morning (6:00 AM - 8:00 AM)**
1. Staff check-in via mobile app
2. Review daily schedule and route assignments
3. Equipment and supply check
4. Depart for first assignment

**During Service**
1. Arrive 5 minutes early
2. Check-in with customer or building manager
3. Complete pre-cleaning inspection (photos via app)
4. Execute cleaning checklist per service type
5. Post-cleaning inspection and photos
6. Customer sign-off via app

**End of Day**
1. Return equipment to supply closet/storage
2. Complete daily report in app
3. Restock supplies for next day
4. Submit any incident reports

### 3.2 Residential Cleaning SOP

**Standard Clean (2-3 hours)**
1. **Entry & Living Areas** (30 min)
   - Dust all surfaces
   - Vacuum/sweep/mop floors
   - Clean mirrors and glass
   - Tidy and organize (customer preference)

2. **Kitchen** (30 min)
   - Clean countertops, backsplash
   - Wipe exterior of appliances
   - Clean sink and faucet
   - Sweep and mop floor
   - Take out trash

3. **Bathrooms** (30 min each)
   - Clean toilet, shower, tub, sink
   - Wipe mirrors and counters
   - Restock toiletries (if provided)
   - Sweep and mop floor

4. **Bedrooms** (20 min each)
   - Dust all surfaces
   - Make beds (if requested)
   - Vacuum/sweep/mop
   - Empty trash

5. **Final Walkthrough** (10 min)
   - Quality check all areas
   - Take completion photos
   - Customer sign-off

### 3.3 Commercial Cleaning SOP

**Office Clean (After hours, 3-5 hours)**
1. **Common Areas** (45 min)
   - Vacuum carpet/mop hard floors
   - Dust furniture and surfaces
   - Clean glass doors and windows
   - Empty trash and recycling

2. **Restrooms** (30 min)
   - Deep clean all fixtures
   - Restock supplies
   - Sanitize all surfaces
   - Floor cleaning

3. **Individual Offices** (15 min each)
   - Desk dusting
   - Trash removal
   - Floor cleaning
   - Supply restocking

4. **Break Room/Kitchen** (30 min)
   - Clean all appliances
   - Wipe counters and tables
   - Sweep and mop
   - Trash removal

## 4. Staff Training Program

### 4.1 Onboarding (Week 1)

**Day 1: Orientation**
- Company culture and values
- Safety briefing
- Equipment overview
- Mobile app training
- Uniform and ID distribution

**Day 2-3: Shadow Training**
- Pair with senior staff member
- Observe full cleaning cycles
- Practice with supervision
- Checklist familiarization

**Day 4-5: Supervised Practice**
- Perform cleans with trainer observation
- Feedback and correction
- Time management training
- Customer interaction practice

**Day 6-7: Assessment**
- Written test (procedures, safety)
- Practical assessment (timed clean)
- Certification upon passing

### 4.2 Ongoing Training

**Monthly (2 hours)**
- New techniques and products
- Customer feedback review
- Safety refresher
- Equipment maintenance

**Quarterly (Half day)**
- Advanced cleaning techniques
- Customer service excellence
- Emergency procedures drill
- Team building

**Annual (Full day)**
- Full recertification
- Industry updates
- Leadership development
- Career path planning

### 4.3 Certification Levels

**Level 1: Junior Cleaner** (0-6 months)
- Basic residential cleaning
- Supervised commercial work
- Standard equipment

**Level 2: Professional Cleaner** (6+ months, assessment passed)
- Independent residential work
- Commercial cleaning
- Specialized equipment

**Level 3: Senior Cleaner** (12+ months, leadership skills)
- All service types
- Train new staff
- Quality inspections

**Level 4: Team Leader** (18+ months, management training)
- Manage team of 5-10
- Schedule coordination
- Customer relations

## 5. Quality Standards

### 5.1 Quality Score System

Each service is rated on a 100-point scale:
- **Thoroughness** (30 points): All areas addressed
- **Attention to Detail** (25 points): Corners, edges, hidden areas
- **Timeliness** (20 points): Completed within allocated time
- **Professionalism** (15 points): Conduct, communication, appearance
- **Customer Satisfaction** (10 points): Post-service rating

**Score Tiers:**
- 90-100: Excellent (bonus eligible)
- 80-89: Good (standard)
- 70-79: Needs Improvement (retraining)
- Below 70: Unsatisfactory (performance plan)

### 5.2 Inspection Process

**Random Inspections** (10% of all services)
- Quality inspector visits unannounced
- 30-point checklist completed
- Score recorded in system
- Feedback provided within 24 hours

**Post-Service Reviews**
- Customer rates service 1-5 stars
- Written feedback collected
- Issues escalated within 1 hour
- Resolution within 24 hours

## 6. Safety Protocols

### 6.1 Personal Safety
- Always wear provided PPE (gloves, mask, goggles)
- Use proper lifting techniques
- Report any injuries immediately
- Buddy system for isolated locations
- Emergency contact on file

### 6.2 Chemical Safety
- Read all SDS sheets before using new products
- Never mix cleaning chemicals
- Proper ventilation required
- Store chemicals in labeled containers
- Spill kit accessible at all times

### 6.3 Equipment Safety
- Inspect equipment before each use
- Report damaged equipment immediately
- Use equipment only for intended purpose
- Unplug electrical equipment when not in use
- Lock out/tag out for maintenance

## 7. Customer Service Guidelines

### 7.1 Communication Standards
- **Professional appearance**: Uniform, ID badge, neat appearance
- **Punctual**: Arrive 5 minutes early
- **Courteous**: Smile, greet, use customer's name
- **Respectful**: Honor customer preferences and privacy
- **Responsive**: Address concerns immediately

### 7.2 Handling Complaints

**LEARN Framework:**
1. **L**isten: Let the customer fully express their concern
2. **E**mpathize: "I understand how frustrating that must be"
3. **A**pologize: "I'm sorry this happened"
4. **R**esolve: Offer specific solution
5. **N**otify: Follow up to ensure satisfaction

**Escalation Path:**
1. Staff member attempts resolution
2. If unresolved, contact team leader
3. If still unresolved, contact customer success
4. Document everything in the app

### 7.3 Customer Retention
- Remember preferences (stored in app)
- Consistent staff assignment when possible
- Proactive communication about schedule changes
- Loyalty rewards program
- Referral bonuses

## 8. Emergency Procedures

### 8.1 Medical Emergency
1. Call 911 immediately
2. Notify team leader and COO
3. Provide first aid if trained
4. Document incident in app
5. Do not return to work until cleared

### 8.2 Property Damage
1. Stop work immediately
2. Notify customer
3. Photograph damage
4. Report via app within 30 minutes
5. Do not attempt repair

### 8.3 Security Incident
1. Leave premises if unsafe
2. Call police if necessary
3. Notify team leader immediately
4. File incident report within 2 hours
5. Do not discuss with media

### 8.4 Natural Disaster
1. Follow local emergency procedures
2. Notify team leader when safe
3. Do not travel to scheduled services
4. Check in daily until clear
5. Document any property damage
