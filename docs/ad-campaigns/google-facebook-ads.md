## Part 3: Ad Creative Guidelines

### Visual Standards
- **Colors**: Primary blue (#0ea5e9), accent purple (#d946ef), white backgrounds
- **Imagery**: Bright, clean spaces; smiling professionals; before/after comparisons
- **Typography**: Clean, sans-serif (Inter), high contrast
- **Style**: Modern, premium, trustworthy

### Video Ad Specs
- **Format**: 9:16 (Stories), 1:1 (Feed), 16:9 (YouTube)
- **Length**: 15s (Stories), 30s (Feed/YouTube)
- **Captions**: Always included
- **Hook**: First 3 seconds critical

### A/B Testing Framework
| Variable | Test A | Test B |
|----------|--------|--------|
| Headline | "First Clean FREE" | "Book in 60 Seconds" |
| CTA | "Book Now" | "Get Started" |
| Image | Clean space | Smiling cleaner |
| Audience | Homeowners | Renters |
| Offer | Free first clean | 20% off |
